Enhancing healthcare for doctors and patients involves simplifying Electronic Health Records (EHRs), reducing administrative burdens, expanding telemedicine, and ensuring accessible Continuing Medical Education (CME) for doctors. Patient-focused improvements include user-friendly portals, health education resources, remote monitoring, and convenient telehealth services. Healthcare apps, cultural competence, patient advocacy, pricing transparency, and feedback mechanisms further empower patients. Implementing these measures fosters a more efficient and patient-centered healthcare system, easing doctors' workloads and offering accessible, high-quality care to patients.
